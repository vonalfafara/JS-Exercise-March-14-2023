About Us

Stronghold Bank is a globally recognized institution with a customer base of over a million individuals, SMEs, and corporations worldwide. The bank has been awarded the Best Bank in the World for ten consecutive years, showcasing its commitment to providing reliable and trustworthy financial services to its customers.

Personal banking services at Stronghold Bank include savings accounts, checking accounts, and time deposits, which provide customers with safe and convenient ways to manage their finances. Savings accounts allow customers to earn interest on their deposits, while checking accounts provide easy access to funds for everyday expenses. Time deposits offer higher interest rates for customers willing to lock their funds for a specific period.

For SME banking, Stronghold Bank offers customized start-up and SME loans to help businesses start or grow their operations. The bank's corporate banking services include long-term loans to invest in business operations and executive services for a more personal relationship with our customers.

With its commitment to serving the needs of its customers, Stronghold Bank has become a trusted institution in the banking industry. The bank's comprehensive range of financial products and services cater to the diverse needs of its customers, making it a go-to financial partner for individuals, SMEs, and large corporations worldwide.

Our History

Since its inception in 1870, Stronghold Bank has been a steadfast pillar of the banking industry. A group of eager young entrepreneurs out of college recognized the need for a reliable and trustworthy financial institution in Davao City. With a vision to build a bank that would stand the test of time, the founding members set out to create an institution that would serve the community with integrity, honesty, and dedication.

In the early years, Stronghold Bank was a small institution, serving the city with basic banking services. However, as the local economy continued to grow, so did the bank. The bank's reputation for providing reliable and trustworthy financial services began to spread, and soon people from all over the Philippines and then the world began to seek out the bank's services.

Throughout its history, Stronghold Bank has weathered many storms. From economic downturns to financial crises, the bank has remained steadfast in its commitment to its customers and to the community it serves. This commitment has earned the bank a reputation as a reliable and trustworthy institution, one that people can count on in good times and bad.

Today, Stronghold Bank is a thriving institution, with branches in cities across all countries in the world. Despite its growth and success, the bank has never lost sight of its roots. The founding values of integrity, honesty, and dedication continue to guide the bank's operations, and its commitment to serving the needs of its customers remains as strong as ever.

Stronghold Bank offers a wide range of financial services to meet the needs of its customers. Its commitment to providing excellent customer service and its focus on building strong relationships with its customers has been a hallmark of the bank's success. Whether in times of growth or adversity, Stronghold Bank has proven to be a reliable and trustworthy partner for its customers.
