Personal Banking

Savings Account

A savings account is a safe and convenient way to manage your finances. It allows you to earn interest on your deposits, withdraw your money at any time, and keep your money secure.

Checking Account

A checking account provides easy access to funds for everyday expenses. It offers features such as check-writing, overdraft protection and low or no minimum balance requirements.

Time Deposit

Time deposit accounts offer higher interest rates compared to regular savings accounts. They require customers to lock their funds for a specific period, usually ranging from a few months to several years. Time deposit accounts are a great option for customers who want to earn more interest on their savings and are willing to leave their funds untouched for a set period.

SME Banking

Start-Up Loan

Stronghold Bank offers start-up loans as a financing option to help entrepreneurs get their business off the ground. It provides the necessary capital to cover start-up costs, such as equipment, inventory, and marketing expenses. Start-up loans typically have flexible repayment terms and competitive interest rates to help SMEs succeed.

SME Loans

Established SMEs may seek financing to help them grow and expand their operations. These loans are flexible and can be customized to meet the unique needs of each business. The bank typically offers competitive interest rates and repayment terms, making it an attractive option for businesses looking for funding.

Corporate Banking

Long-Term Loan

Long-term loan is a financing option are available to large corporations to invest in their operations, expand their business, or finance major projects. These loans have low-interest rates and longer repayment terms, making it an attractive option for companies seeking substantial capital. The bank may also offer customized repayment schedules based on the unique needs of the business.

Executive Services

Stronghold Bank offers customized and exclusive services for corporate executives, including treasury management and cash management services. Our team of experienced professionals will work closely with executives to provide tailored solutions to meet their unique financial needs. With our executive services, executives can focus on managing their business while we take care of their financial operations.
